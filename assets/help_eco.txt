Help for Pembantu's Economy features

> ?Work
    no further context required, gives the author money, 1 hour cooldown

> ?Shop
    no further context required, shows the shop

> ?balance
    no further context required, shows the author's money

> ?Withdraw [amount]
    transfers [amount] coins from the bank, to the user's wallet

> ?Deposit [amount]
    transfers [amount] coins from the wallet, to the user's bank

> ?Buy [item] [amount, default = 1]
    buys [amount] [item] with the money from the wallet

> ?Bag
    no further context required, shows a user's items
